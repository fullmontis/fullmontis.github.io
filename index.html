Terms

Payment

  • Payments only through Paypal in Euro
  • Only upfront payment. Half start/half end only for large commissions (greater than €200)

Copyright

  • I reserve copyright of the artwork commissioned, and you as the commissioner are completely free to repost and reuse the artwork for any noncommercial purposes. On my part, I will honor any request to not redistribute the commissioned work if that is your wish.
  • For commercial purposes, a commercial license must be agreed on and purchased.

Slots

  • Commission are reserved by slots. A slot is reserved when the full agreed price for the commission is paid. In particular cases a down payment can be used to reserve a slot; however, the commission will start only when the full payment is completed.

Commission

  • A commission can take from a few days to a few weeks from the day of the payment. Average time is about a week. Weekly updates are given to keep you up to date with the progress of your commission.
  • I need to have a precise mental image of what you want before I start drawing, for this reason I will ask plenty of questions and ask for references. If I'm unable to get a clear idea before starting drawing then this is probably a design commission, and as such requires an additional fee to be applied depending on the commission
  • It is not possible to change rendering styles mid commission. E.g. it is not possible to change a commission from "single color render" to "full render", or viceversa. This is due to differences in style workflows

Revisions

  • Revisions are allowed. Major revisions are allowable only in the sketch phase; once the sketch is accepted only revisions of smaller details are allowed.
  • Only two revisions are allowed, one for the sketch and one for the final work. Multiple issues can be and must be addressed with each revision.
  • Additional charges may be added to the commission for revisions after the two mentioned above, or if major modifications of the work done so far are required.

Refunds

  • Partial refunds are allowed only while the commission is in progress; once the final illustration is completed refunds are allowed only in very specific cases
  • I reserve the right to deny/cancel any commission at any time. A full or partial refund is going to be given in case that happens, depending on the progress of the commissions.

FAQ

How can I contact you?

Send me an email. I answer to all messages I receive on any platform, but email is the only thing I have notifications for so I may miss your message otherwise.

How can I support you?

If you like my content and you'd like to support me, the simplest way to do so is simply sharing my content to spread the love! You could also commission an illustration, or donate on ko-fi.

Where I can get more information about your OCs?

I have a section in my discord server that goes more in depth about my characters (still a work in progress though).

What software/tablet do you use for drawing?

All my illustrations are made in Krita on a Wacom Cintiq 16.

Can you suggest me a drawing tablet?

I am not the best person to ask this, I am not well informed on tech products. But I'd say buy the biggest tablet that fits your budget, size is probably the most important factor.

Can I repost your illustrations?

Yes, please link to the source if you do. However this is only valid for my own pieces, you cannot repost commissioned pieces.

Where do you find your references?

I use reference mostly for lighting, studying and inspiration, but other than that I almost always draw from imagination.

Do you take free requests?

No, sorry.

Can I draw fanart of your OCs?

Of course! Only thing I'm asking is no NSFW/nudity please!

Help, I'm struggling with my art! Can you give me some advice?

I'm always happy to help! Get in touch and I'll see what I can do.
