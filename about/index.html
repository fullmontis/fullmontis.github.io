About fullmontis

I am an italian hobbyist illustrator, writer and game developer. My main focus is on telling stories and crafting experiences.

Why this site?

It is mostly an aggregator for all the stuff that I do. Juggling around too many social media and mediums was stressful, and to keep my sanity I needed a reference point. I still use social media, but they are subordinate to the website now.

What's with the weird name?

It's a nickname that I've been using for a while. I like it because it is almost never taken when I register to a new website.
