In this game you are a girl that is trying to save his sick brother from death. You need to sacrifice your life and skills and manage your resources carefully.

This is a simple platformer that I had originally made in 48 hours for the Ludum Dare 43 competition (here is the original compo page). I improved on the basic mechanics by using the feedback I got in the compo during December, and released it on Christmas.

It is my first attempt at making something I would actually enjoy playing, and it seems to have worked out in the end. The two endings allow for some replayability, and the gameplay itself, while simple, is engaging and challenging enough to be still fun to play.
